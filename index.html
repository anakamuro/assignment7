Coco Lemonade

Coconut milk: 400ml lemon juice: 2 Coconut cream: 4 cuilleres Sugar: 20g Ice Cubes: 2

10 min

Put the ice cubes in the blender, add the milk, coconut cream, the juice of 2 lemons and the sugar. Blend until you get the desired texture

Tahitian raw fish

Tuna: 200g Cucumber: 1 Tomato: 2 Carot: 1 LIme: 5 Coconut milk: 100ml

60 min

Dice the tuna, place in a dish and cover with lime juice (it is better to take a wide and shallow dish). Leave to rest in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours...

Reunionese coconut chicken

Chicken: 1 Coconut milk: 400ml Tomato sauce: 25cl Onion: 1 Red peper: 1 Olive oil

80 min

cut the chicken into pieces and brown them in a casserole dish with olive oil. Season with salt and peper. Once browned, cook by adding water. After 20 minutes add the tomato sauce...
