Coco Lemonade  10 min
Coconut milk: 400ml
lemon juice: 2
Coconut cream: 4 cuilleres
Sugar: 20g
Ice Cubes: 2
Put the ice cubes in the blender, add the milk, coconut cream, the juice of 2 lemons and the sugar. Blend until you get the desired texture
